Using the Lathe.

The lathe is controlled by using the square and circular markers.

The top marker on the axis line can only move on the axis.
The bottom marker on the axis line is fixed.

The circular markers control the shape of the lathe blade (the line joining the two markers).

Further adjustments to the blade can be made by adding new markers.
Click anywhere on the blade and a dialogue box opens, so you can do this.


This dialogue box can also be used to straighten or curve sections of the blade and to delete them.
