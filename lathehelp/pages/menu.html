Menu


The Lathe was originally made to produce new Cubees for the Stretch and Squash Cubee App (To Be Constructed).
Hence the container around the turned shape.
Bringing up the menu will allow you turn the container off using Free Size.

In the Stretch and Squash Cubee App you can set the stretch and squash range to
  • One Cubee as used in the Just Cubees App
  • A Mini Cubee which is one quarter of a Cubee
  • A Micro Cubee which is one sixtieth of a Cubee

When the range is set to one Cubee shapes within the app can only be moved by one Cubee at a time.
The container gives an idea of the spacing that will occur between shapes.
Think of them as showing the margins for the shape.

Free Size shows the size of the turned shapes in units.
