Viewing Area

Here you get and eye level view of the scene and you can fly throught it using the numeric keypad.
Press ESC to return to the Scene Room.
  • 5 move forward
  • 8 move up
  • 2 move down
  • 0 move backwards
  • 4 turn towards the left
  • 6 turn towards the right
  • 7 or 9 turn upwards
  • 1 or 3 turn downwards
  • + increase pace
  • - decrease pace

Forward movement is always towards the direction that you are looking at, backwards is the opposite direction.
Small white crosshairs show where you are looking.

The amount you can turn upwards and downwards is limited.

All models in the scene are solid. You can get close to them but not through them. Turns can also be blocked by the models.
