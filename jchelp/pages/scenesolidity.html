Scene Solidity

When Solidity is ticked Cubees in the model will act as solid objects they will not pass through each other or the walls or the ground.
When Solidity is not ticked Cubees in the model pass through each other and the walls and the ground.

There is an issue when the cursor keys are held down and models are fast moving even with Solidity ticked they can pass into each other.
If this happens you may have to untick Solidity to free the model then re-tick Solidity.
Single presses on the keys avoids this issue.
