Solidity

When Solidity is ticked Cubees will act as solid objects they will not pass through each other or the walls or the ground.
When Solidity is not ticked Cubees pass through each other and the walls and the ground.

When Solidity is ticked angled Cubees will not join neatly.

When Solidity is not ticked angled Cubees will join neatly.

There is an issue when the cursor keys are held down and Cubbes are fast moving even with Solidity ticked they can pass into each other.
If this happens you may have to untick Solidity to free the Cubees then re-tick Solidity.
Single presses on the keys avoids this issue.
