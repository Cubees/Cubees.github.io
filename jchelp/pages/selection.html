Selection Sub Menu.

Selecting a Cubee.
Click a Cubee to select. Shift and click to select more than one Cubee.

Rotation.

Clicking on each icon will rotate the selected Cubees through 90 degrees about an axis through the middle of the Cubee that joins the two colours.

Clear, Select All, Copy and Delete do as they say.


The currently active colour is shown to the right of the colour selection table.
