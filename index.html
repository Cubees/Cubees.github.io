Just Cubees Plain and Simple
What is a Cubee?
Imagine you have lots of invisible cubes all the same size.

A Cubee is a shape that just fits inside one of these cube. By fitting Cubees together you can make models and by placing models you can make a scene.

In the workshop there is a room for constructing models and one for building a scene which you can view as a walkthrough.

The Workshop is a basic introduction to building with just Cubees. You cannot change the size of a Cubee nor make your own Cubees
